About Our Department

Discover our journey of excellence in computer science education and research

Our Mission

The Computer Science & Engineering Department is committed to providing world-class education, conducting cutting-edge research, and fostering innovation in the field of computing. We aim to develop skilled professionals who can contribute to technological advancement and societal progress.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized department that leads in computer science education, research, and innovation, producing graduates who excel in their careers and contribute meaningfully to the technology landscape.

Department History

Established in 1985, our department has grown from a small program with 30 students to one of the premier computer science departments in the region. Over the years, we have consistently adapted our curriculum to meet industry demands and technological advancements.

Academic Programs

B.Tech Computer Science

4-year undergraduate program covering fundamentals of computer science, programming, algorithms, and software engineering.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Intake: 120 students
  • Specializations: AI/ML, Cybersecurity, Web Development

M.Tech Computer Science

2-year postgraduate program focusing on advanced topics in computer science and research methodologies.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Intake: 60 students
  • Research Areas: AI, Data Science, Systems

Ph.D. Program

Research-intensive doctoral program for advanced study and original research in computer science.

  • Duration: 3-5 years
  • Research Focus: Cutting-edge CS topics
  • Full-time and Part-time options
