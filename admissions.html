Admissions

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and integrated programs. Explore the admission details below.

Undergraduate Programs (B.E CSE)

Eligibility

Pass in Higher Secondary Examination with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

Duration

4 years (8 semesters)

Intake

120 Students

Postgraduate Programs (M.E CSE)

Eligibility

B.E/B.Tech in CSE/IT or equivalent from a recognized university.

Duration

2 years (4 semesters)

Intake

18 Students

Integrated Programs (M.Tech CSE)

Eligibility

Pass in Higher Secondary Examination with strong foundation in Mathematics and Physics.

Duration

5 years (10 semesters)

Intake

30 Students

How to Apply?

Admissions are based on entrance exams and merit as per the university guidelines.
