Prerequisites

Below are the degree-specific prerequisites required for pursuing various programs in the Computer Science and Engineering Department.

Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering

  • Master’s degree in CSE/IT or equivalent with minimum 60% marks or CGPA as per university norms.
  • Strong research background with published papers in reputed journals/conferences preferred.
  • Qualifying exam such as GATE/UGC-NET/CSIR or institutional entrance test.

B.E. Computer Science and Engineering

  • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate of 50%–60% (varies by category as per regulations).
  • Valid score in entrance exams such as JEE Main/TNEA or equivalent.

M.E. Computer Science and Engineering

  • Bachelor’s degree in CSE/IT or relevant discipline with minimum 60% marks or CGPA.
  • GATE/TANCET or equivalent qualifying examination score.
  • Good academic record and strong fundamentals in programming and algorithms.

M.Tech Integrated in Computer Science and Engineering

  • Completion of 10+2 with Mathematics and Physics as mandatory subjects.
  • Strong aptitude in problem-solving, programming, and logical reasoning.
  • Admission based on entrance exam or merit-based selection process.
